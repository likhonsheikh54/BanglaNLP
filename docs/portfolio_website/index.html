Featured Projects

BanglaNLP

NLP Machine Translation Dataset Creation

BanglaNLP is a comprehensive toolkit for creating high-quality Bengali-English parallel datasets from news sources, designed to improve machine translation and other cross-lingual NLP tasks for the Bengali language.

Key Achievements:
  • Created the largest open-source Bengali-English parallel corpus (120K+ pairs)
  • Developed novel hybrid alignment algorithm with 92% accuracy
  • Implemented multi-stage quality filtering pipeline
  • Featured in multiple research papers on low-resource MT

Publications & Research

BanglaNLP: A Large-Scale Bengali-English Parallel Corpus from News Sources

2023 | Conference on NLP Applications

We present BanglaNLP, a comprehensive toolkit and the largest open-source Bengali-English parallel corpus derived from news sources. Our work addresses the critical gap by providing a dataset of 120,000+ sentence pairs across diverse topics.

Intelligent Alignment Techniques for Low-Resource Parallel Corpora

2023 | Journal of Computational Linguistics

About Me

I'm Likhon Sheikh, an NLP researcher and developer focused on creating language technologies for underrepresented languages.

With a background in computer science and linguistics, I've dedicated my career to bridging the digital language divide through innovative NLP solutions. My work on BanglaNLP represents my commitment to ensuring Bengali speakers have access to the same language technologies available to speakers of more well-resourced languages.

My research interests include cross-lingual transfer learning, low-resource machine translation, and NLP for social good. I believe that language should never be a barrier to accessing information and opportunities in the digital age.
